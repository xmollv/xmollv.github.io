Later: Save Links for Later

Later is the easiest way to save links, articles, and videos to read or watch later. Stay organized and never lose track of what matters.

Later is completely private, with no ads or tracking. Your data is stored securely on iCloud, accessible only by you on all your devices (iPhone, iPad and Mac).

  • Completely private
    Your data lives on iCloud, only you have access. No servers involved.
  • No ads nor tracking
    Enjoy Later with zero ads and no tracking of your activity.
  • Native apps for iPhone, iPad and Mac
    Seamless experience across all your Apple devices.
  • Safari Extension for macOS
    Save links directly from Safari on your Mac.
  • Share Extension for iOS and macOS
    Quickly save content from any app using the share menu.
  • Import and export from multiple sources
    Easily move your saved links in and out of Later.
  • Daily backups
    Your data is automatically backed up every day.
  • Pay once, unlock everywhere (iPhone, iPad & Mac)
    Either one-time purchase or subscriptions, use the one that makes the most sense to you.

About the Developer:

Later was created in 2020 by Xavi Moll, an independent developer who wanted a read-later app that was simpler, easier to use, and completely private. Because there are no ads nor any sort of tracking, Later relies solely on user subscriptions to keep the lights on.
